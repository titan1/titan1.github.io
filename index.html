Biography of Albert Einstein

Biography

"Albert Einstein was born on March 14, 1879, in Ulm, Germany. He developed the theory of relativity, one of the two pillars of modern physics."

Famous Quotes

Quote 1: "The important thing is not to stop questioning. Curiosity has its own reason for existing."

Quote 2: "Imagination is more important than knowledge. For knowledge is limited, whereas imagination embraces the entire world."

Quote 3: "Life is like riding a bicycle. To keep your balance you must keep moving."
